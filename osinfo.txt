OS Info
=======

All standards-compliant OSes shall provide information about themselves through the `osinfo` library.  This library shall have the following fields:

  - `osinfo.hostname: string`: The current system hostname or 'localhost'.
  - `osinfo.osname: string`: The name of the operating system.
  - `osinfo.libraryPath`: Where libraries are generally stored.
  - `osinfo.`
